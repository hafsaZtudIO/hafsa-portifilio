My Educational Journey

I believe education is the key to success and personal growth. Here's my story:

Primary and Secondary Education

I started my formal education in 2014 at Al Huda Primary School in Hargeisa, where I studied from Grade 1 to Grade 8, graduating in 2022.

Currently, I am a Form 3 student at Al Huda Secondary School, working hard to balance my studies and personal growth.

Quranic Studies

Alongside my formal education, I attended a Quranic madrasa and completed the memorization of the Quran in 2019. Alhamdulillah, this has been one of my proudest achievements, and I am grateful to Allah for His guidance.

Diplomas and Lifelong Learning

I have also taken part in various diploma programs, including one in Social Work, and others that have enriched my knowledge and skills. Now, I am balancing my secondary school studies with college-level courses as I prepare for 2024 and beyond. Currently, I am studying at Tanaad College and am close to completing my graduation.
